Why It Matters

Would you want your investments supporting what you pray against? Most Christians have simply never been given the choice.

Behind the ticker symbols in most funds are companies doing things you may pray against — and most broad-market funds quietly hold at least a few.

Biblically Responsible Investing closes that gap. We are stewards, not owners — and how you deploy God’s money is worship, not just math.

How BRI Works

Three ways your money can honor God

BRI isn’t just about what you avoid. It’s a proactive strategy to put your capital to work for good.

Avoid

Thoughtfully screen out companies whose primary business conflicts with biblical values, such as abortion, pornography, and addiction.

Affirm

Invest with conviction in companies that serve people, strengthen families, and reflect biblical values in how they operate.

Advocate

Use shareholder engagement to be a positive voice for biblical values where business decisions are made.

And no, faith and returns are not a trade-off. Leading biblically screened funds have delivered performance in line with the S&P 500.3

Common Questions

Answers before you even ask

Will you make me feel bad about what I currently own?

Never. Every portfolio was built before you knew what was in it. This is about your next step, not your last one — and we’ll treat it that way.

Will I sacrifice returns by investing biblically?

Values-aligned portfolios are built with the same discipline around diversification, cost, and risk as any portfolio we manage, and some leading biblically screened funds have performed in line with broad market benchmarks (sources in the footer). Past performance is not indicative of future results, and any screening approach can perform differently than the broad market.

What exactly is the “What Do I Really Own?” Review?

A live review of your current holdings during your free discovery meeting. We look inside the funds you already own, show you which companies and activities your dollars are supporting, and walk through what a values-aligned alternative could look like. There’s no cost, and the findings are yours to keep whether or not we ever work together.

What if I decide this isn’t for me?

Then you’ll leave with a clearer picture of your finances than you came with, and we’ll part as friends. Truly — the review is valuable on its own, and it’s yours either way.

Do I have to move all my accounts to get started?

No. Many families start with a single account — an old 401(k), an IRA, or a brokerage account — and expand from there only if it’s a good fit for them.

How much does it cost?

Discovery meetings are always free — take as many as you need. If we end up working together, there are three clear ways to start: free DIY tools, a $1,500 one-time plan, or full-service management from 1.35% or less per year. Everything is explained in plain English and in writing before you commit to anything.

Do I have to be a Christian to work with you?

Not at all. We serve anyone who wants thoughtful, values-aligned planning. We simply specialize in helping Christians whose deepest desire is for their portfolio to reflect their faith.

We don’t live near Newington — can we still work with you?

Yes. We’re based on the New Hampshire Seacoast and meet with families in person across NH and New England — and virtually with clients around the country. Distance is never the obstacle.
